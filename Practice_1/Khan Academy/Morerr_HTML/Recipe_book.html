Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Heat a pan and pour the batter.

Step 3: Cook until golden on both sides.

Fresh Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1–2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 1 bowl
Tomato 1
Cucumber 1

Step 1: Chop all vegetables.

Step 2: Mix in a bowl.

Step 3: Add dressing and serve.
